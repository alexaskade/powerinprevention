I created a list of various approaches and ultimately decided to focus on a new crop of parents who are trying to keep their child’s best interest at heart but gets easily swayed by the fear and mostly untrue rhetoric of the anti-vaxx groups.


week8

Presented by first prototype/experimentation,

which was essentially brand strategies for two different brands, a fear-based non-profit, PIP, and a luxe lifestyle blog? brand, Lucame. I created a 3-phase strategy of the brand and then a basic visual identity for both. I did not feel strongly about this first prototype. I still researched aggressively on my own to develop a better understanding of what I want to do.
